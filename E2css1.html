Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices bacon
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 8 slices bread

Instructions:

1. Cook bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Drain on paper towels.

2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of half of the bread slices. Top with bacon slices and tomato slices. Place another slice of cheese on top, then cover with the remaining bread slices, buttered side facing out.

4. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place sandwiches in the skillet and cook until golden brown on each side and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.

5. Serve hot and enjoy!
